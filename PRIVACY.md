# Privacy Policy

**HN - Hacker News Reader**
**Last updated: April 6, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how HN - Hacker News Reader ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what is necessary for the App to function.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us:

- **Bookmarks** — stories you save for later
- **Read history** — story IDs you have already viewed
- **Upvotes** — story IDs you have upvoted
- **Theme preference** — your chosen appearance setting (light, dark, or sepia)

This data lives in your device's local storage. We have no access to it, and it is removed when you uninstall the App.

### 1.2 Hacker News Account Credentials (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with your Hacker News account:

- Your **username and password** are sent directly to `news.ycombinator.com` over HTTPS to authenticate you. We do not receive or store your password.
- Your **username** is stored locally on your device to keep you signed in across sessions.
- Authenticated actions (upvoting, favoriting) are performed by sending requests directly to `news.ycombinator.com` on your behalf.

Signing in is entirely optional. The App is fully functional without an account.

### 1.3 Crash Reports and Error Logs

We use **Sentry** (sentry.io) to collect crash reports and error logs. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Sentry may collect:

- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Stack traces and error messages at the time of a crash

This data does not include your name, email address, or any content you have viewed. Sentry's privacy policy is available at [https://sentry.io/privacy/](https://sentry.io/privacy/).

### 1.4 User Feedback (Optional)

If you choose to submit feedback through the in-app feedback form, you may optionally provide your name and email address. This information is transmitted to Sentry and used solely to respond to your feedback. Submitting feedback is entirely voluntary.

### 1.5 Search Queries

The Search tab uses the **Algolia HN Search API** (hn.algolia.com). Search queries you enter are sent to Algolia's servers to return results. Algolia's privacy policy is available at [https://www.algolia.com/policies/privacy/](https://www.algolia.com/policies/privacy/).

### 1.6 Hacker News Content

Story and comment data is fetched from the public **Hacker News Firebase API** (hacker-news.firebaseio.com). No personal information is sent in these requests.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information (unless you voluntarily provide it in the feedback form)
- We do not serve advertisements
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not use tracking pixels or third-party analytics SDKs beyond what is described above
- We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location

---

## 3. Data Retention

- **Local data** (bookmarks, read history, upvotes, theme) — retained on your device until you clear it in Settings or uninstall the App
- **Crash and error reports** — retained by Sentry according to their data retention policies
- **Feedback submissions** — retained until you request deletion

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App displays content from Hacker News, a public technology news forum intended for general audiences.

- **Delete all local data** at any time via Settings → Clear Cache
- **Sign out** at any time via Settings → Sign Out, which removes your stored username
